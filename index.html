Welcome

MUTIC is a prototype programming language for making music and exploring music theory. It uses parameterised generators to produce sequences which are either constant, or change over time. This makes it easy to create music that 'mutates' and allows complex textures and patterns to be realised with minimal code. The interpreter is small, flexible and self-contained.

(rng 1000)

(= nn01 (E2 A3 C3))
(= nn02 (+ C1 (flt (+ nn01 (choose 16 (C0 C0 E0 G0 G0 C1 C1 E1))))))

(= nd00 (choose 32 (1 1 2)))

(= tracks (
    (track arp 0 nn01 120 (choose 32 (1 2)))
    (track arp 1 (rot 1 nn02) 100 nd00)
    (track arp 1 (rot 2 nn02) 100 nd00)
))

(sound font FS3)
(sound channel 0 GM2.AcousticBass)
(sound channel 1 GM2.AcousticGuitarNylon)

(sound start 60 tracks)
